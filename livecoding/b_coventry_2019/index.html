Coventry Algorave

 

At Coventry’s first ever Algorave, on July 16th 2019 at the Herbert Art Gallery and Museum, Antonio Roberts (hellocatfood) and I (mxwx) live coded music collaboratively using Troop and TidalCycles, with Carol Breen (imagemover) on visuals. This was my first ever public live coding/algorave performance. We performed among other excellent live coders, Lucy Cheesman (Heavy Lifting) and Innocent.
